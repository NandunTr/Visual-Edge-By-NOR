We are Visual Edge by NOR

Founded by Nandun Teekshana Ratnayake, Visual Edge by NOR is a creative studio dedicated to capturing life's most precious moments with an artistic eye and cinematic precision.

From intimate wedding ceremonies to grand corporate events, from stunning product photography to eye-catching graphic designs — we bring your vision to life with passion and professionalism.

Every frame we capture and every design we create tells a unique story, blending modern aesthetics with timeless elegance.
